# Loom Script — Dataleap Quickstart Hub Demo

**Target length: 2 minutes**
**Tone: Confident, direct, zero filler — every sentence earns its place**

---

## Opening (10 seconds)

"Hey Jan — I'm Niran. I'm applying for Chief of Staff, and instead of a resume, I built three things for Dataleap."

## Problem + Hub Demo (50 seconds)

*Screen share: Vercel app landing page*

"The biggest growth lever for Dataleap right now is activation — getting new users to their first agent fast. Zapier solved this with a template directory that drives 8.5 million monthly visitors. No one in the AI agent space has built a real version of this."

*Click to /templates, filter by Sales*

"So I built one. Twenty-five agent templates, organized by role, each with copy-paste-ready instructions that work directly in Dataleap."

*Click into Pre-Call Brief Generator, show the instructions, click Copy*

"A user can grab these, paste them in, and have a working agent in minutes."

*Navigate to /advisor, show the welcome message*

"There's also an AI advisor — answers a few questions about your role and tools, then recommends templates. No competitor has this."

## Dataleap Agents (25 seconds)

*Switch to Dataleap tab*

"I also built three agents on the platform — a competitor intel digest, a content repurposer, and an onboarding advisor. Each took about 20 minutes. Here's a real output."

*Flash the competitor intel email output*

## Close (20 seconds)

"I connected all of this in a growth playbook — linked below. I built everything in a few hours because this is how I work: fast, autonomous, focused on what moves the needle. I'm ready to help Dataleap become the default way teams work with AI."

---

## Before You Hit Record

- Two browser tabs ready: (1) Vercel app on landing page, (2) Dataleap dashboard with agents
- Clean desktop, no clutter
- Pick one specific detail from your agent output to flash on screen
- Test that filters, detail page, and copy button work
- If AI Advisor is live, have the welcome screen showing

## While Recording

- Don't read — internalize the structure, speak naturally
- Click through things WHILE talking, don't pause to narrate
- One take, no re-dos — confidence > perfection
- Smile on the open and close
